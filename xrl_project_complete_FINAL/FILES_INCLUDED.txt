SIMULATION RESULTS SUMMARY
================================================================================

Energy Sweep (0.5 - 5.0 keV):
  - 20 data points computed
  - Peak contrast: 1.000 at 0.50 keV
  - Optimal range: 1.0-2.0 keV (balance contrast/absorption)

Gap Sweep (1 - 50 μm):
  - 20 data points computed
  - Contrast degrades 0.993 → 0.387 over range
  - Recommended: 10-20 μm for sub-micron features

Absorber Thickness (0.1 - 1.0 μm Ta):
  - 20 data points computed
  - Contrast increases 0.123 → 0.606
  - Transmission decreases 91.7% → 42.0%
  - Optimal: 0.5-0.8 μm

Resist Comparison:
  - PMMA: High resolution, 500 mJ/cm² sensitivity
  - ZEP520A: Best balance, 80 mJ/cm², low LER
  - SU-8: Negative, high aspect ratio capable
  - HSQ: Ultra-high resolution, 800 mJ/cm²

Thermal Analysis (0.1 W beam):
  - Si3N4: 21.6 μm deflection, 0.16 MPa stress
  - Diamond: 9.4 μm deflection (BEST for high power)
  - SiC: 34.7 μm deflection, higher stress tolerance
  - Polyimide: 469 μm deflection (UNSUITABLE)
