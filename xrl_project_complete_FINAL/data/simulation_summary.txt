RECOMMENDATIONS:
----------------------------------------------------------------------
• **Mask Material**: Au provides better contrast than Ta at 1.5 keV, leading to slightly better resist features (comparison in plots).
• **Resist Selection**: ZEP520A and HSQ demonstrate superior LER control compared to PMMA, making them essential for sub-100 nm features.
• **Dose Control**: CD is highly sensitive to dose factor, necessitating precise dose delivery, especially for low-contrast masks (see PMMA/Ta sweep).
• **Thermal Management**: Diamond offers vastly improved thermal stability, keeping deflection well below the 0.1 μm tolerance even at 1 W beam power.
