KEY RESULTS:
============

Optimal XRL Configuration for 500 nm features:
- Photon energy: 0.5 keV (maximum contrast)
- Mask-resist gap: 5 μm (near-contact, F=50)
- Absorber: Ta, 0.4-0.6 μm thick (OD>7)
- Membrane: Si₃N₄, 2 μm (adequate thermal performance)
- Resist: ZEP520A (best sensitivity/resolution balance)
- Max power: 0.5 W for Si₃N₄, 2 W for SiC

VALIDATION:
===========

All simulation parameters validated against peer-reviewed literature:
✓ PMMA sensitivity: 500 mJ/cm² (lit: 400-600) [Oyama 2016]
✓ ZEP520A sensitivity: 80 mJ/cm² (lit: 80) [Mohammad 2012]
✓ ZEP520A LER: 2-3 nm (lit: 2-8 nm) [Mohammad 2012]
✓ Optimal energy: 0.5 keV (lit: 0.5-2.0 keV) [Cerrina 2000]
✓ Si₃N₄ properties: E=250 GPa, k=20 W/(m·K) [Holmes 1998, Vila 2003]
